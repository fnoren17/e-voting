Electoral rolls in paper voting

In the Swedish voting system, all eligible voters receives a voting card by mail before the election day. The voting card comtains the adress to your polling station, contact information if you have any questions and your number in the electoral roll. At the polling station, the voting card and an ID is necessary.

Electoral rolls in e-voting

Electronic voting systems also have electoral rolls, and users need to authenticate themselves by signatures, password or one-time code in order to vote.

Voter choose a ballot paper, then put the ballot papers into envelopes. The envelopes are placed in the ballot box, so that people could not see others' votes or easily add or remove ballot papers.
